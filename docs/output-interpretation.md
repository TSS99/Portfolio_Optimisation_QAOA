# Output Interpretation

The notebook produces two main outputs near the end.

## Printed probabilities

The printed list shows the top portfolio bitstrings and their probabilities. This is useful for quickly identifying the most likely candidates.

## Bar chart

The bar chart shows the probability distribution visually. This is useful for seeing whether the optimized circuit strongly prefers one portfolio or spreads probability across many choices.

## What to report

In a project report, include:

- the most likely bitstring;
- what that bitstring means as selected assets;
- whether the distribution is concentrated or spread out;
- any parameter changes that affected the result.

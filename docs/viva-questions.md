# Viva Questions

These questions are useful for oral review or project evaluation.

1. Explain the full workflow from portfolio data to final bitstring.
2. What does `101` mean in this notebook?
3. Why does the project use a covariance matrix?
4. What happens if the risk-aversion parameter is increased?
5. Why are constant terms ignored in the Hamiltonian?
6. What is the purpose of Hadamard gates at the start?
7. What do `gamma` and `beta` control?
8. Why is COBYLA suitable for this small demo?
9. Is QAOA guaranteed to find the global optimum?
10. How would you make the project more realistic?

Strong answers should connect the finance objective, binary optimization, and QAOA circuit instead of explaining them as disconnected topics.

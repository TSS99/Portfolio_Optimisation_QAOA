# Contributing

Contributions should keep the project beginner-friendly.

Good improvements include:

- clearer explanations of QAOA steps;
- small experiments that students can run;
- comments that explain non-obvious code;
- fixes for setup or dependency issues;
- comparisons with simple classical baselines.

When adding new material, prefer short sections with direct examples. The goal is for college students to follow the logic without needing advanced quantum computing background first.
